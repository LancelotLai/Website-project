Gundam Seed Destiny (GSD) is my favourite Gundam Anime when I was a child.
Compare with all others Gundam anime, it is the most sentimetal one.
Friendship, Love, Wrath, Homeland.
All things are also influenced by war and will be described in this website.


Although Kira & Arthun are friends,
because of war, they need to fight with each other.


Although Shinn & Stella love each other,
because of war, they cannot have a good ending.


Although Kira was just wanted to stop the murder,
because he killed Stella in the war, Shinn takes revenge on him.


Although Cagali is weak,
even it is war, she also want to protect her country - Orb.

Friends

When I was child, I throught peoples' friends are also should be their comrades.
But when I was watching Gundam Seed Destiny, it has provided me a new perseption.
Our friends may not same as our comrades. But if my friend is my enemey, it is very cruel for ourseleves.

Kira and Arthun are friends. Different point of view on the war caused them fight with each other, even both are not wrong.
It is a cruel act to fight with friends in the battlefield.

Lovers

Ofcourse, when I was a kid, I didn't really understand what is love.
The person you want to play with? Maybe this was the anwser whild I was a child.
However, one thing I certainly remember which is Gundam Seed Destiny changed my love peception.
Since I was fantasizing if the lovers love each others, they must have a good ending when I was child.

Stella and Shinn. Although they love each other, they could not have a good ending.
Stella is dead. Although Shinn still alive, the sorrowful fact leads him plan to revenge.

Revenge

Shinn is extremly angery because of Stella death. Fortunately, there is a chance for him to revenge.
As Kira won't shoot mobile suit's cockpit, and Impulse Gundam can change the body parts while it is battling.
Finally Shinn defeated Kira.
When I was child, revenge this word is just for mischief.
But Shinn let me felt that revenge should be a behavior which is more important and serious.
I was really throught it is very cool when I was child.

Homeland

Which place should be counted as home? The house you live in? The district? Or the state?
Cagali has demonstrated the answer. She loves her state - Orb.
She want to protect her home country, even she is very weak, even her authority has lost.
I was impressed by Cagali's passion, speech, spirit and action when I was child,
especially while she takes akashuki to protect Orb.
